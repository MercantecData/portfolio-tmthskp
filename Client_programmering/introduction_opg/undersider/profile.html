Tommy Munk Thomsen

About me:
My name is Tommy.
I was born in 1982 and have in my life time completed several jobs and educations.

As an 23 year old lad I got my Highschool diploma. Since then I have tried: classical drawing artist , painter, Webintegrator, media graphic designer, and worked as a factory worker.

I am originally from a small area in Denmark called “Thy”. It lies very close to the beautiful shore of the North sea of Jutland in Denmark. Even thou im originally from “Thy” I have also lived several places in Denmark, because of easyer access to jobs and educations.

Interests:
I have always been very creative, and always been happy to use this ability. I have been like that even since i was in kindergarden.

In kindergarden I prefered to sit inside with my pencils, drawing comicbook heroes. Insted of going outside and play soccer with the other kids from the kindergarden.

On top of my creative hobbys i also have a healthy interest fitness and healthy foods. I especially like strength workouts.
