Pencil Drawing

Pencil Drawing is the oldest and purest form of art in my Opinion. All who even thinks about starting some kind of art hobby or work should also train in the pencil drawing craft.

I say this because if one can draw with pencil, then transfering the same skills developed in pencil art, will make all other types of art easyer and faster to learn.
